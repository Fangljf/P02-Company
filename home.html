As unique as yourself

A collection of unique one of a kind handmade pottery, dinnerware, gifts and more for intentional living

CRAFTING COMFORT

We firmly believe in crafting pieces that are timeless so they can elegantly transition between generations. We're not interested in adding to landfills and are committed to making sure your ceramics last you a lifetime.

Designed to be comfortable enough for everyday use and fancy enough for dinner parties.

AS UNIQUE AS YOURSELF

All our handmade ceramics are crafted with intention and mindfulness in our Vancouver studio. Every piece is one of a kind and made from locally sourced clays from the Pacific Northwest. Only lead-free and food safe glazes are used that are formulated and tested for durability and longevity.
